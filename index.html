About Me

The youngest of three boys and only son not qualified as Dr. Trembley. I've worked in the food service industry for the past 13+ years in a variety of positions: banquet caterer, late-night pizza cook, country club bartender, and fine-dining service.

All things must come to an end, and the end of my serving career came in late 2019. I decided to take the plunge on Vanderbilt's Full-Time Web Development Bootcamp and am just beginning my journey as a web developer. This is my first responsive website!

I've always been fascinated with history, poltical theory, and economics; however, these are topics that often divide us (especially in an election year -- yucky). I draw inspiration that undeniably unites us from man's best friend.
